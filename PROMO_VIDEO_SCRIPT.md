# AI Prompt Generator: Promotional Video Script & Context

**Goal:** Create a fast-paced, high-energy 45-60 second promotional video for Product Hunt, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn.
**Vibe:** Sleek, modern, problem-solving, and premium.
**Key Message:** Stop getting generic AI responses because of bad prompts. Turn lazy ideas into expert-grade execution prompts instantly—and automatically route them to the perfect AI model.

---

## 🎬 1. The Hook (0:00 - 0:08)
*The goal is to instantly grab attention by showing a problem everyone relates to.*

- **Visual:** Screen recording of someone typing a terrible, lazy prompt into ChatGPT (e.g., "build me a fitness app"). ChatGPT spits out a generic, boring, bulleted list.
- **Text Overlay (Big & Bold):** "Stop getting generic AI answers."
- **Voiceover/Text:** "We all know AI is powerful... but if you write a lazy prompt, you get a lazy response."
- **Visual Transition:** Fast zoom or swoosh into the dark, sleek UI of the **AI Prompt Generator**.

## 🚀 2. The Solution: Guided Mode (0:08 - 0:25)
*Showcase the core magic of the app.*

- **Visual:** The user types that same lazy idea into the AI Prompt Generator: `"build a fitness app that tracks reps with the camera"`. 
- **Action:** Cursor clicks the thick, premium **"Guided Mode"** button.
- **Visual:** The UI smoothly expands. The AI asks 3 incredibly smart, multiple-choice questions (e.g., "What expert role should the AI assume?", "What is the specific camera tracking logic?"). The cursor clicks the answers.
- **Voiceover/Text:** "Enter a vague idea, and the engine’s Guided Mode will ask you exactly the right questions to sharpen your intent."

## ✨ 3. The Reveal: Master Prompt (0:25 - 0:35)
*The payoff. Show the massive prompt it builds.*

- **Visual:** Cursor clicks **"Generate Prompt"**. The loading spinner finishes.
- **Action:** A massive, structured, expert-grade RTCFC (Role, Task, Context, Format, Constraints) prompt appears on screen. 
- **Text Overlay:** "Expert-grade prompts, generated in seconds."
- **Voiceover/Text:** "In seconds, your lazy idea is transformed into a master-level execution prompt."

## 🧠 4. The Kicker: Model Routing (0:35 - 0:50)
*This is the unique selling point (USP) that separates it from just being a prompt builder.*

- **Visual:** Scroll down to the **Model Recommendations** section. Show the tabs clicking between: *Open Source*, *Freemium*, and *Premium*.
- **Action:** The screen highlights the "Reasoning" text for why the system picked *Claude 3.5 Sonnet* or *Llama 3.3 70B* for this specific coding task.
- **Voiceover/Text:** "But we don't stop there. The engine analyzes your task and tells you exactly which AI model on earth is best suited to run it—giving you direct links to Open Source, Freemium, and Premium platforms."
- **Visual:** Cursor clicks the "Copy & Open Platform" button. A new tab snaps open directly to the recommended AI.

## 🍉 5. The Outro & Call to Action (0:50 - 1:00)
*Leave a lasting impression.*

- **Visual:** The camera pans up to show the sleek UI, resting on the Watermelon 🍉 solidarity banner at the top of the screen.
- **Text Overlay:** "Try it for free today."
- **Voiceover/Text:** "Stop wasting time engineering prompts. Let AI do the engineering for you. Try it now."
- **Visual:** Final screen shows the App Logo (🍉), the URL, and the "Available on Product Hunt" badge.

---

## 🛠️ Recording Tips for the Editor

1. **Resolution:** Record in at least 1080p (preferably 4K) so the text in the generated prompts looks crisp.
2. **Aspect Ratio:**
   - **For Twitter/Product Hunt:** Use 16:9 (Landscape Desktop recording).
   - **For TikTok/Reels/Shorts:** Record the Mobile View (9:16). The app is highly optimized for mobile, so showing the sticky bottom action bar sliding over the keyboard will look incredibly slick on Shorts!
3. **Pacing:** Speed up the video by 1.5x during the "waiting/loading" spinners. People on social media have zero attention span.
4. **Music:** Use an upbeat, modern synth-wave or lo-fi electronic track. It should sound "techy" but premium.
